Greek Chicken Meatballs

Home

Description

Delicious Greek Chicken Meatballs are full of flavor, easy to make, and perfect for a weeknight dinner. Serve the meatballs with a generous scoop of homemade tzatziki and your favorite veggies!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground chicken (or turkey)
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • Serve with: chopped romaine, white rice, cucumber, tomato, feta, tzatziki sauce

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients and choice of meet to a bowl and mix until combined.
  2. Roll into meatballs. Cook meatballs in skillet with olive oil.
