Loco Moco

Home

Description

Three layers of rice, hamburger patty, and an egg smothered in a savory gravy. One serving is enough to make anyone have a kanak attack.

Ingredients

  • Gravy:
    • 1 1/2 cup beef stock
    • 5 tsp cornstarch
    • 4 tsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 2 tsp ketchup
  • Hamburger patty:
    • 1 1/2 lb ground beef
    • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 1/2 tsp seasoning salt
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 small onion sliced
  • 3/4 cup mushrooms
  • 1-2 tbsp butter
  • Sunny side-up eggs (one per serving)
  • Rice
  • Scallions (chopped)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients for gravy and set aside.
  2. Mix all ingredients for hamburger patties and form into patties (3/4-1 inch thick).
  3. Heat pan on medium heat and cook patties for 3-5 mins on each side.
  4. Set each patty on a piece of paper to catch excess grease.
  5. In the same pan, melt 1 tbsp of butter. Once butter is melted, add onions & cook for 1 min.
  6. Add mushrooms & cook for 2 mins.
  7. Melt 1 more tbsp of butter (optional) & pour in gravy sauce. Cook & stir until sauce is thickened.
  8. Assemble rice, patty, sauce, egg & scallion for garnish.
